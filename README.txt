ticTacConnect is a crossover between connect 4 and tic tac toe.
inner boards are connect 4, whoever wins the inner board wins a spot in the outer game of tic tac toe
outer boards are tic tac toe, whoever wins the tic tac toe game wins

you get to choose the dimension of the baord and how many pieces are needed to win each board
+ means the grid was chosen
/ means grid was tied

python3 ticTacConnect.py to compile
have fun
